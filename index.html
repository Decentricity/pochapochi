Pocha Pochi

Pocha Pochi is a social room app using GunDB, a peer-to-peer decentralized database. Create a room, share the invite link, and play or chat directly with people who have the encrypted URL.

🔒 End-to-end encrypted – Only people with the invite link can read messages.

Start a room
1. Open the app
2. Pick a display name
3. A new room link is generated automatically
4. Use Share Link to invite someone
5. Messages stay encrypted end-to-end
Join a room
1. Open the full invite link you received
2. Keep the #k= fragment intact
3. Pick a display name for this room
4. Wait for relay connection
5. Start chatting
